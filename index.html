    • Research

    Isogeometric analysis (IGA), first introduced by T.J.R. Hughes et al., is a numerical technique that aims to bridge the gap between computer-aided design (CAD) and finite element analysis (FEA). I'm interested in developing the theory and efficient methods of spline-based parameterization, which is a fundamental step in IGA and has a great effect on the subsequent analysis.
